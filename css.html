Medium rare rib-eye steak recipe

Listen to medium-rare steak recipe:

Preparation

  1. Take the Steak out of the fridge for roughly 30 mins before cooking
  2. Whilst waiting, prepare your frying pan and get garlic powder, thyme, salt and pepper ready to flavour the steak
  3. After 25 mins, take out a frying pan and place it on the hob heated at 180°C
  4. Pour olive oil onto the frying pan and allow it to heat up for a few minutes

The cooking process

  1. Wash your hands with soap for at least 20 seconds
  2. Put the steak into the frying pan
  3. Sprinkle the curry powder, thyme, salt and pepper onto the upwards facing steak
  4. Leave it to fry for around 4 mins at 180°C
  5. Flip over the steak and allow it to fry
  6. Repeat stage two on the now upwards facing steak
  7. Allow to fry for around 4 mins at 180°C on this side
  8. Whilst waiting for it to fry, cut any vegetables you might want to eat with the steak
  9. Now, remove the steak from the pan, put it on your plate and you're ready to go!
